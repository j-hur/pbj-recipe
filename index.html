Samyang Buldak Noodles

Let's learn how to cook buldak spicy noodles

Ingredients

  • The Noodles
  • Spicy Sauce

Instructions

  1. Pour water into a pot and turn the stove on
  2. When the water boils, put the noodles in
  3. Cook for 5-6 minutes
  4. Taste the noodles to see if they are ready, and then drain the water out
  5. Pour the sauce in and enjoy!
  6. PS: Grab some milk because it's SPICYYYY
